Our Story

POLLYANNA CONSULTANCY SERVICES was established in 2019 with the aim of providing quality guidance to students who intend to study. Today, we are the leaders in educational consultancy and represent leading colleges and universities in India.

Pollyanna consultancy services is a point of contact for students to reach universities and colleges. It empowers students by giving them up to date information on latest job trends, top colleges and supports them in admission procedure providing them guidance in all necessary aspects from career counselling, towards enrollment, educational assessment, accommodation for the period of education etc.

We are Direct Representation of Top-Ranked 1000+Colleges and Universities and tie up with top Training Academy and also tie up with top country leading companies for placement.

Quality education promotes competency of employees and also results in overall growth and advancement of the society.

By, Md sajid akhter, Management director
